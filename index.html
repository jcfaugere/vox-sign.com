About VOX

VOX is a post-quantume signature algorithm submitted to NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography Project on November 30th, 2017. It has been designed by: Benoît Cogliati, Jean-Charles Faugère, Pierre-Alain Fouque, Louis Goubin, Robin Larrieu, Gilles Macario-Rat, Brice Minaud and Jacques Patarin.

VOX is based on multivariate cryptography (UOV problem and the problem of solving algebraic equations).
